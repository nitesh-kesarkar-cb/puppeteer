Recommended Actions

Profile

  • Noticeable weight loss or reduced intake.
  • Nutrition-impact symptoms (nausea, early satiety, taste changes).
  • Early signs of muscle or fat loss, reduced function.

Intervention Recommendations

  • Refer to a dietitian for individualized support.
  • Promote small, frequent meals enriched with energy and protein.
  • Introduce ready-to-drink nutritional products tailored to specific clinical needs (e.g., high-protein, fiber-enriched, diabetes-appropriate, oncology-adapted).
  • Manage barriers to intake (symptom relief, texture/flavor adjustments).
